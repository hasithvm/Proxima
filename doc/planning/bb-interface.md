*Description*

This document contains the required pins for the CC3200-Mainboard interface.

*Required Pins*
1. Power 2x(3V3, GND) (4)
2. SPI-master (3 CSes/MISO/MOSI/CLK) (6)
3. I2C-master (SCL/SDA) (2)
4. SPI-slave (/CS) (1)
5. Control (CTRL) (1)
6. GLCD GPIO (1)
7. Interrupts (6)
8. UART (RX/TX)
9. Interrupt Out (1)

Total pins: 24
